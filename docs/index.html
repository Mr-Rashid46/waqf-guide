UMEED Portal Hub
(اُمید پورٹل)

The UMEED Portal (Unified Management of Electronic Waqf Data) is a national digital system by the Ministry of Minority Affairs and the Central Waqf Council for Waqf property registration and transparency.

Mandatory Document Checklist
(لازمی دستاویزات)

All Waqf trustees and Mutawallis (managing Mosques, Madrasas, Graveyards, Khanqahs, etc.) must prepare the following verified documents before registering on the UMEED Portal.
(تمام وقف اداروں کے ذمہ دار حضرات درج ذیل کاغذات تیار رکھیں تاکہ امید پورٹل پر رجسٹریشن آسانی سے ہو۔)

Legal & Registration Documents

(قانونی و رجسٹریشن دستاویزات)

  • Waqf Board Registration Certificate
    Proof of official registration under State Waqf Board
  • Order Letter
    Issued by Waqf Board / Authority
  • Jodh Patra (Attachment Document)
  • Committee Resolution (if applicable)
Property & Land Records

(جائیداد و زمین کے کاغذات)

  • Waqf Deed
    Legal ownership and purpose document
  • 7/12 Extract or Land Record
    Revenue record showing property ownership
  • Form D (Farfaar)
    Mutation record after changes or transfers
  • Sale / Purchase Deed
    If land was purchased or transferred
  • Property Photographs
    Front, boundary, and inside view
Financial & Audit Records

(مالی و آڈٹ دستاویزات)

  • Audit Report
    Latest financial statement verified by auditor
  • Income & Expenditure Statement
  • Ensure all records are up to date
  • NOC / Consent Letter (if shared property)

⚠️ Last Date for Registration: 5 December 2025

Failure to register your institution on the UMEED Portal before the deadline may lead to up to 6 months imprisonment or a fine between ₹20,000 and ₹1,00,000.
(اگر ۵ دسمبر ۲۰۲۵ تک رجسٹریشن نہیں کیا گیا تو ۶ مہینے کی قید یا ۲۰,۰۰۰ سے ۱ لاکھ روپے تک کا جرمانہ ہو سکتا ہے۔)

Frequently Asked Questions
(عمومی سوالات)

The UMEED (Unified Management of Electronic Waqf Data) Portal is a digital platform launched by the Government of India to register, monitor, and manage Waqf properties across the country in a transparent and efficient way.

Mutawallis, Trustees, and authorized Waqf Committees managing Waqf properties can register on the portal to upload and verify details.

Registration should be done only after official announcements and guidance from your local Waqf Board or Jamiat Ulama office. Please do not register before the declared date.
